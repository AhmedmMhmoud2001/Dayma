Know Our Story

We provide premium ingredients for your cooking journey

a leading company in agro commodities trading through b2b channels is set to enter the b2C market with a premium position, focusing on delivering only the best quality of pantry goods to the consumer with a new brand, Dayma, that will enter the market with an ecofriendly proposition

Sustainability

Our planet provides nutritious food to keep our bodies healthy, that’s why we are committed to giving back by protecting it and keeping it healthy.

We are glad to say that our packaging material is ecofriendly. It consists of paper and biodegradable plastic. The composition of our packaging material is an innovation that was only achievable through hard work and extensive collaborations with our partners.

Our range of herbs and spices are packed in a sustainable packaging, glass jars and tin cans

Innovation

We are glad you liked our packaging! Do you want to know more about the innovation?

Kraft paper is our main packaging material however we had to add a layer of biodegradable plastic in order to have a transparent window that will allow the consumer to check the premium quality packed inside every pack. We worked with our production line manufacturer on a solution that would provide a sustainable packaging solution.

Quality

Mediterraneo the mother company and a leading company in agro commodities trading, sources the best crops. Our range of products go through strict sorting procedure in one of the three state of art facilities owned by Mediterrneo. After sorting out the best beans and grains, all products get delivered to Dayma’s facility where they go through a final sorting phase and finally packed to the consumer. Hygiene is one of our key values and that’s why we have strict policies implemented to avoid any contanmination.
